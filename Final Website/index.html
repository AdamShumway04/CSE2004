Team History & Legends

A timeline of iconic Spurs eras — click an era to expand.

George Gervin, “The Iceman,” turned scoring into an art form. His silky finger roll and effortless buckets helped cement the Spurs as a permanent part of the NBA landscape, putting San Antonio on the basketball map long before the championship years.

Guided by Tim Duncan, Manu Ginóbili, and Tony Parker, the Spurs became a model of unselfish, beautiful basketball. Five championships, a legendary coach in Popovich, and a culture built on humility and excellence defined this era.

Victor Wembanyama brings size, skill, and creativity the league has never seen in one package. The Spurs are once again in the spotlight, blending their historic culture with a futuristic superstar built for the modern game.
